Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie

30 min - Prep time

10 min - Cook time

40 min - Total

8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tbsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons,juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks,beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust,baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven : Prehear oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling : In Medium saucepan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornstarch,and salt
    • Stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium-higher heat,stirring frequently,until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter
    • Place egg yolks in asmall bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make Meringue In large glass or metal bowl...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add Sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake : Bake in preheat oven for 10 minutes,or until meringue is golden brown.
